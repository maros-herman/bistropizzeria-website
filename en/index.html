Our philosophy

Three secrets of our taste

Every pizza that leaves our oven carries with it centuries-old tradition and love for the craft.

Dough made from Caputo flour

We use exclusively Italian tipo 00 flour from the Caputo family from Naples. We leave the dough to ferment for 48 hours for perfect crispness and lightness.

San Marzano tomatoes

Our sauce is made from DOP certified San Marzano tomatoes from the sunny region of Campania. Sweet, intense, authentic.

Wood oven

Our wood oven reaches a temperature of 450 °C. The pizza is baked in just 90 seconds – just like the masters in Naples do.
